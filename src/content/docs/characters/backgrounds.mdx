---
title: Backgrounds
---

Backgrounds tell you about your lifebefore you decided---or were forced---to change your path and become an adventurer. 

1. Choose a background, or roll d66 (roll 2d6 and treat the first dice as the first digit, and the second dice as the second digit). 
2. Roll or choose in column 3 to see what change in your fortunes set you on your new path. 
3. Add the items to your inventory.
